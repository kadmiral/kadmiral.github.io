What kadmiral is

Kubernetes management

SWe'll deploy, update and configure kubernetes for you, in your chosen cloud provider. No strings attached.

Developer Dashboard

Easy to use dashboard and cli that abstracts kubernetes deployments. One place to create, deploy, rollback, configuration, and read logs.

Opinionated solution

We figure out the best settings for kubernetes and supporting open source apps. And although we'll leave some room for custom configuration where possible, this is not a rule.

What kadmiral is not

PAS

Although you can argue that we are a PAS-like solution, everything works in your chosen cloud providers.

Consultancy

kadmiral deploys to your infrastructure. All the process is automated, all the infrstructure is yours. kadmiral support will help you solve problems only in the clusters we deployed.

Silver Bullet

If your app a billion dollar social network or a wolrdwide known streaming platform, we might not be the best fit for your main platform. But we can help a lot by handling all your more standard apps while you focus your precious ops and devs to your core business apps.
